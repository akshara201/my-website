AI in a Sustainable World

Role of AI

Artificial Intelligence plays a major role in promoting sustainability by improving efficiency and decision-making in areas like energy, agriculture, transport, and climate research. AI helps monitor environmental changes, manage natural resources, predict climate patterns, reduce pollution, and automate recycling and waste management.

Advantages

  • Increases energy efficiency and reduces waste
  • Supports real-time environmental monitoring
  • Enhances disaster prediction and response
  • Optimizes logistics to reduce carbon emissions
  • Enables precision agriculture and smart farming

Disadvantages

  • High energy consumption for AI training
  • Job loss
